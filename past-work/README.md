# past-work/ — your already-submitted assignments

**This is how Claude learns to write like you.** Drop in 5–10 of your previously submitted papers, discussion posts, or essays — ideally a mix, ideally the ones that sound most like you.

On first run, Claude reads everything here and writes `voice-profile.md` (your tone, sentence rhythm, vocabulary, how you cite). The more real samples you add, the closer the match. Raw/authentic work matters more than heavily-polished pieces.

> Privacy: the **contents** of this folder are git-ignored by default (see `.gitignore`) so your real graded work is not published to GitHub. The folder structure stays; your files stay local unless you choose to track them.
